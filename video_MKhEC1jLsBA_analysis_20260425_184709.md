Based on the video, here is a detailed list of the features shown in the Wisery digital contact card app, including their UI elements and functionality:

### 1. Pre-designed Templates
*   **UI Elements:** A grid layout displaying various visual previews of business card designs. The templates are categorized into pairs of "Personal online card" and "Wallet card".
*   **Functionality:** Users start their journey by selecting a template that fits their brand or style. This provides a starting point that can be fully customized later.

### 2. Online Card Customization Editor
*   **UI Elements:** A split-screen interface. The left panel contains various settings and input fields categorized by sections (e.g., Online card URL, Core info, Image, Button design). The right panel displays a real-time, interactive preview of the digital business card as changes are made.
*   **Functionality:** This is the core design tool for the web-based version of the business card.
    *   **Custom URL:** Users can define a personalized URL for their card.
    *   **Core Info:** Text fields to input and update essential details like Name, Title, and Company.
    *   **Image Upload:** An interface to upload, crop, and position a profile picture or company logo.
    *   **Button & Icon Design:** Controls to change the shape (square, rounded, pill) and color of action buttons and social media icons using a color picker.
    *   **Theme/Background:** Options to set the background of the card to a solid color, an uploaded image, or a color gradient.
    *   **Typography:** Settings to adjust the font family, size, and color of the text on the card.

### 3. Modular "Blocks" System
*   **UI Elements:** A menu on the left side listing "Added blocks" and "Available blocks" (like Buttons, Wallet card, Collect contacts, Social media links). Users can seemingly add, remove, or rearrange these elements.
*   **Functionality:** Allows users to add specific interactive features to their online card.
    *   **Buttons:** Add custom links with specific calls to action (e.g., "Create your business card").
    *   **Wallet Card Block:** Adds a button to the online card that allows visitors to download the contact information directly into their phone's native digital wallet (Apple Wallet or Google Wallet).
    *   **Collect Contacts (Lead Capture):** Adds a feature that turns the card into a lead generation tool, allowing visitors to submit their own contact information back to the card owner.

### 4. Wallet Card Customization Editor
*   **UI Elements:** Similar to the online card editor, it features a split-screen with settings on the left and a live preview of the digital wallet pass (e.g., Apple Wallet format) on the right.
*   **Functionality:** Specifically designs the version of the card that lives inside a smartphone's digital wallet app.
    *   **Information Fields:** Input areas for general information and specific contact details (phone, email, address) that will appear on the front and back of the wallet pass.
    *   **Images:** Upload areas for a small logo and a larger hero/banner image.
    *   **Background:** A color picker to change the primary background color of the wallet pass.
    *   **QR Code Generation:** The system automatically generates a unique QR code linked to the card.
    *   **Export Options:** Buttons to directly "Add to Apple Wallet", "Add to Google Wallet", or download the QR code as a PNG or SVG file for print materials.

### 5. Mobile Scanning and Lead Capture (User Experience)
*   **UI Elements:** The native camera app on a smartphone scanning a QR code displayed on another phone's digital wallet. This opens a mobile web browser displaying the "Online Card".
*   **Functionality:**
    *   **Sharing:** A user opens their wallet card and presents the QR code. Another person scans it to instantly access the online profile.
    *   **Lead Capture Form:** On the online card, the visitor taps "Share your contact with me". This opens a clean, mobile-optimized form with fields for First Name, Last Name, Email, Phone, and a toggle to join a mailing list. Upon submission, the data is sent to the card owner.

### 6. Native Contact Saving (User Experience)
*   **UI Elements:** A "Save to contacts" button on the mobile online card. Clicking it brings up a summary modal, and confirming the action downloads a standard contact file (like a .vcf).
*   **Functionality:** This file automatically opens the phone's native "New Contact" screen (e.g., iOS Contacts app). All the information from the digital card (Name, Phone, Email, Company, URLs, Address, and even custom notes) is pre-filled, allowing the user to save the new contact directly to their phone's address book with one tap.

### 7. Contact Management Dashboard
*   **UI Elements:** A clean, tabular interface within the web app showing a list of collected leads. Columns include Name, Phone, Email, Position, and Company.
*   **Functionality:** Acts as a lightweight CRM.
    *   **Lead Storage:** Automatically stores the information submitted by people who filled out the "Share your contact with me" form on the digital card.
    *   **Data Management:** Users can view their collected contacts and use the "Export CSV" button to download the entire list for use in other marketing or sales software.
