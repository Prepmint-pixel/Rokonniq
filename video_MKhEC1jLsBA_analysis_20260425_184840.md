Here's a detailed breakdown of the features, UI elements, and functionality demonstrated in the Wisery platform video:

### 1. Template Selection
*   **Feature:** Pre-designed Templates
*   **Description:** When a user starts, they are presented with a grid of pre-designed templates.
*   **Functionality:** Users can select a matching pair of designs for both their "Personal online card" (web-based) and "Wallet card" (Apple Wallet pass). These act as a starting point and can be modified later.

### 2. Online Card Customization
*   **Feature:** Core Information Editing
*   **Description:** Fields to update personal and professional details.
*   **Functionality:** Users can input their name, job title, company, and other essential details that will appear on the digital card.
*   **Feature:** Image Upload & Editing
*   **Description:** A tool to add a profile picture or logo.
*   **Functionality:** Users can upload an image, and a built-in slider allows them to zoom and crop the image to fit the designated area.
*   **Feature:** Button Design Customization
*   **Description:** Options to style the call-to-action buttons on the card.
*   **Functionality:** Users can choose the button style (Solid, Outlined, or Text) and use a color picker to change the button color to match their branding.
*   **Feature:** Theme Customization
*   **Description:** Tools to change the overall look of the online card.
*   **Functionality:** Users can change the background color using a color picker, switch to an image background, or apply a color gradient. There are also pre-set theme options available at the bottom of the sidebar.

### 3. Key Blocks (Content Modules)
*   **Feature:** Add Buttons
*   **Description:** Customizable call-to-action links.
*   **Functionality:** Users can add buttons that link to any URL (e.g., website, social media, booking page) and customize the text on the button.
*   **Feature:** Wallet Card Block
*   **Description:** A module to share the wallet pass.
*   **Functionality:** Adds a section to the online card that allows visitors to easily download or access the user's Apple Wallet card.
*   **Feature:** Collect Contacts (Lead Capture)
*   **Description:** A form to gather information from people who view the card.
*   **Functionality:** Adds a "Share your contact with me" button. When clicked, it opens a form where the visitor can input their details, turning the digital card into a lead generation tool.

### 4. Wallet Card Design
*   **Feature:** Wallet Pass Customization
*   **Description:** A dedicated interface for designing the Apple Wallet pass.
*   **Functionality:** Users can update general and contact information specifically for the wallet pass, upload a logo or profile image, and change the background color of the pass.
*   **Feature:** QR Code Generation
*   **Description:** A unique QR code linked to the user's card.
*   **Functionality:** The system automatically generates a QR code. The UI provides options to download the QR code as a PNG or SVG file, and an "Add to Apple Wallet" button to save the pass directly to a device.

### 5. Mobile Interaction (Live Demo)
*   **Feature:** QR Code Scanning
*   **Description:** The primary method of sharing the card in person.
*   **Functionality:** A user opens their Apple Wallet to display their Wisery card and QR code. Another person uses their smartphone camera to scan the QR code, which instantly redirects them to the user's online card in their web browser.
*   **Feature:** Two-Way Contact Sharing
*   **Description:** The process of a lead sharing their info back.
*   **Functionality:** On the online card, the person who scanned the code can tap "Share your contact with me." This opens a form where they enter their First Name, Last Name, Email, Phone, and Company. Upon submission, a success message is displayed.
*   **Feature:** Save to Contacts
*   **Description:** Adding the Wisery user's details to the phone's native address book.
*   **Functionality:** The person viewing the card taps "Save to contacts." This downloads a vCard file, automatically opening the native iOS "New Contact" screen with all the Wisery user's information (name, phone, email, website, social links, address, and notes) pre-filled and ready to save.

### 6. Contact Management
*   **Feature:** Contact List Dashboard
*   **Description:** A backend table storing captured leads.
*   **Functionality:** Whenever someone fills out the "Collect Contacts" form on the digital card, their information is automatically saved to this dashboard. The table displays Name, Phone, Email, Position, and Company, and includes an "Export CSV" button to download the data.
